Same concept as Duolingo, focuses a lot more on vocabulary

LingoDeer (mobile only): https://www.lingodeer.com/

Lingvist: https://lingvist.com/
In case you need more vocab practice

Tinycards: https://tinycards.duolingo.com/
Flashcards to help brush up on concepts and vocab from Duolingo courses

Put your computing power to good use

Earn some pocket change

Be careful of some of the offers on these sites, don’t give out any identifying personal info to anything that looks suspicious. I do recommend these sites though. Ask me or /r/beermoney for any tips with these sites.
(in order of recommendation)

Prolific Academic: https://www.prolific.ac/p?ref=ZAR78H08
Take part of academic studies put out by colleges, pays well compared to other sites listed

Tips:

  • Save until £20 and use Circle Pay for no fee cash out
  • These require your complete attention once you start, don’t start if you don’t have time / have to leave for some reason. It is timed.
  • Check throughout the day as studies flash in and out quickly.
  • Make sure you verify your account, so you can get paid.
  • Make sure you do all your prescreening so you can get studies to do.

InstaGC: https://www.instagc.com/1915057
Offer wall site, easy to earn fast, gift cards are actually received instantly (as per the name)

Tips:

  • Best hyprMX payout, easy
  • Peanut Labs surveys are alright

Microsoft Rewards: https://rewards.microsoft.com/
Check in everyday, search on Bing, slow but really easy

Tips:

  • Make sure to check on your “Daily set” everyday to earn a point streak, increasing in rewards for every day.
  • Check your “Points Breakdown” for everything you can do.
  • Reaching Level 2 every month helps a ton. Lets you earn more points.

EarnHoney: www.earnhoney.com/home/?referrer=11E795E861
Pays you to watch videos

Tips:

  • Throw this on an idle computer, occasionally needs you to check that it’s still running.

SwagBucks: http://www.swagbucks.com/p/register?rb=42398246
Another offer wall like InstaGC, offers pay somewhat well

Tips:

  • On your bar at the top of the page, “Watch These Videos & Earn Big” for 5SB is usually very easy to do.
  • The to-do list on the left side of the home page is usually easy to complete as well

Perk: perk.com/r/f57cd6ba
Watch videos, better earning on your phone

Tips:

  • Perk TV and Perk TV Lives apps let you earn passively.
  • Rewards Reader app pays out well compared to others, but requires you to actively check every article.

Earnably: https://earnably.com/i/nicsstuffandthings
Yet another offer wall

Tips:

  • Engagemetv / Smorestv pays best here, requires some babysitting though (having to check that it’s still going). Use the 0.8 point offers.
